Reduce

Reducing is the idea of using less products than what you normally do. Purchasing less leads to less waste.


    Tips

  • 1. Maintain and repair appliances and goods at home so there is no need to purchase new.
  • 2. Purchase products and goods that use less plastic packaging to reduce waste.

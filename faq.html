Have a look below and read some of the more common questions. If the question you have does not show up below please send it to us in the space above and we will get back to you as soon as we can.


1. What happens when you put everything in one recycling bin?

Before you had to seperate trash into specific bins but now most facilities use technology like magnets, electric currents and lasers to sort all the trash.


2. Can aluminium foil be recycled?

Yes it can be recycled but not all recycling facilities will accept it. Some of the reasons that facilities wont accept it are that it is to thin or that it is covered with to much food.


3. Is glass recyclable?

Yes glass can be recycled. It may however be rejected if it is broken. Glass is a great option to reuse for multiple purposes.
